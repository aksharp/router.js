Registering multiple routes and routing to them

Sequential routing

You can make sure that synchronous routes are routed in order by writing them out one after another

Example:
route.to('/some/route');
route.to('/another/route');

However, if your route function is asynchronous, route resolution order can be ensured using jQuery promises. See jsFiddle below:

Routes on URL

URL will look something like this: index.html#/your/route You can navigate directly to registered route's url and it will be resolved

Navigation & History

Routes are stored in your browser history and you can navigate back and forth using your browser's back and forward buttons
